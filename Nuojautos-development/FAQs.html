FAQs

Where do you ship to?

We ship worldwide.

When will I get my order?

Items are made to order to reduce unnecessary waste. Usually the whole process of manufacturing and shipping takes 5 to 7 business days.

What inks do you use?

We use water-based and eco-friendly inks. We use a special solution in the printing process, so don't worry if you notice a slight new shirt scent or some discolouration. It will disappear after the first wash.

I have a question about my order. Who can I contact?

If you have a question about your order, please email us at info@nuojautos.com

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payment from debit or credit cards - American Express, VISA, Mastercard, Maestro, Cirrus, as well as payments made by PayPal and Apple Pay.
